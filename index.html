My Remarkable Works

During my studies I worked on lots of different projects and below you'll find some of my favorite projects i worked on this year.

GUTS Redesign

GUTS is a digital ticketing company that uses blockchain to make tickets more secure and easy to use. I made a complete redesign for GUTS and focused on improving the online waiting queue. GUTS uses blockchain technology to issue honest tickets that put an end to disgraceful secondary market prices and ticket fraud. Our digital tickets come with countless innovative benefits for fans and organizers that you won’t get anywhere else.

Heineken Procurement Game

During my time at RMMBR I designed this procurement game. The goal of the game is to gather points. Points are gathered by playing quizzes and answering questions about buying raw materials. The more points you gather the higher you get in the leader board. Not enough points? Challenge a coworker for a duel and gamble your points. But watch out! If your proponent wins he gets the points you gambled.
